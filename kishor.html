Kishor Kumar

Kishore Kumar, original name Abhas Kumar Ganguly, (born August 4, 1929, Khandwa, British India—died October 13, 1987, Bombay [now Mumbai]), Indian actor, playback singer, composer, and director known for his comic roles in Indian films of the 1950s and for his expressive and versatile singing voice, which, in the course of a career that spanned nearly four decades, he lent to many of India’s top screen actors.
